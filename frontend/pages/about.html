About Us

We are two students from the College of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Al al-Bayt University, united by one shared vision: to make healthcare smarter and more accessible.

We built ClinicalMind, a platform that leverages the power of artificial intelligence to assist healthcare professionals in making accurate medical diagnoses.

Our goal is to help medical professionals discover insights faster, improve diagnostic accuracy, and provide better patient care through intelligent technology.

We believe that technology can understand people, not just data.

Every line of code we write is driven by our passion to make medical diagnosis fairer, faster, and more insightful.

Our Mission

To leverage artificial intelligence to assist healthcare professionals in making accurate diagnoses, ultimately improving patient care and outcomes.

Our Vision

A world where AI-powered medical intelligence is accessible to healthcare providers everywhere, enabling faster and more accurate diagnoses.
