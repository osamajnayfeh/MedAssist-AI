Frequently Asked Questions

Is ClinicalMind a replacement for doctors?
No, ClinicalMind is designed to assist healthcare professionals, not replace them. Always consult with qualified medical professionals for diagnosis and treatment.
How accurate is the AI diagnosis?
Our AI models are trained on millions of medical cases and provide high accuracy. However, results should always be verified by medical professionals.
Is my data secure?
Yes, all patient data is encrypted and protected. We follow strict privacy and security protocols to ensure data safety.
What image formats are supported?
We support common medical image formats including JPEG, PNG, and DICOM files.
Can I use this on mobile devices?
Yes, ClinicalMind is fully responsive and works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
